Cultural and Historical Heritage

Pakistan boasts a cultural heritage that spans millennia, influenced by ancient civilizations such as the Indus Valley Civilization, as well as the legacies of the Islamic period, the Mughal Empire, and British colonial rule. This diverse cultural tapestry is reflected in Pakistan's languages, arts, architecture, cuisine, and religious practices, with Islam being the predominant religion. The country celebrates a variety of festivals and traditions that showcase its cultural vibrancy and unity amidst diversity.
